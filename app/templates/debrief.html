What This Study Was About

This study investigated whether the format (modality) in which an AI recommendation is delivered affects the quality and consistency of human supply chain decisions.

You were randomly assigned to one of two groups:

  • Group A — received recommendations as: Numerical Statistics → Visual Dashboard → AI Narrative
  • Group B — received recommendations as: AI Narrative → Visual Dashboard → Numerical Statistics

You were in Group {{ group }}.

We hypothesise that groups who see numerical statistics first (Group A) will make more precisely calibrated decisions, while narrative-first groups (Group B) may show greater variance in outcomes. Results from all participants will be analysed and submitted for publication.
