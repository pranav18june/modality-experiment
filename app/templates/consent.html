About this study

This study investigates how different ways of presenting AI recommendations affect supply chain inventory decisions. You will evaluate a series of supply chain disruption scenarios and decide how to adjust two inventory parameters.

  • Duration: approximately 45–55 minutes
  • Task: 12 inventory adjustment decisions across 3 stages, with short rating scales between stages
  • Incentive: All participants receive a goodie bag on completion. Top 10% performers in each group receive a ₹500–1,000 cash award and certificate.

Your rights

Participation is voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without penalty. Your responses are anonymised — no personally identifying information is stored beyond the demographic data below. Data is used solely for academic research and will not be shared outside the research team.

This study has been approved by the Manipal University Jaipur Institutional Review Board.
